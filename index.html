- Who Is Vittorio? -

My name is Vittorio Pugliese and I am a young man from Buenos Aires who loves to investigate and learn about computing by himself. My first steps were around 2010 when I wanted to start making music on my computer. Since that time, I always wanted to learn and do new things by my own.

The first contact I had with coding was in 2019, in the last year of High School we were learning Pseudocode, but it's not until the last months of 2020 that I started to learn JavaScript.
In this moment I have a solid base into pure JS, but now I'm learning React.js to build scalable websites and make bigger projects.

I am a person who learns easily and is not afraid to ask if I'm doing something wrong. The next step I want to make is learn Next.js to start deploying professional websites.
Feel free to contact me and ask any question you have.
